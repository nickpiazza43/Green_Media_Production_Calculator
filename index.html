"Green Media Production" - a calculator


Green Media Production is an upcoming topic in the industry and has become more important for companies, especially in public broadcasting.

One way or another, most of them do not know how to calculate their carbon footprint. This calculator is not complete, but it gives a little insight of what amount of CO2 to expect from a film set. It may be higher depending on the production and their needs.
